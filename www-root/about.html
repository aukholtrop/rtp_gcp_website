rtp-gcp.org is a user group started in 2018. The GDG name is GDG Cloud RTP. Each week we have a one to two hour workshop. During these workshop we

  • sometimes just chat
  • work on projects
  • free labs provided by Google/Qwiklabs or AWS
  • periodically we participate in Google campaigns such as DevFests, StudyJams or Fireside Chats.

For more info about our meetings visit the official GDG page here
